About Us

A Family Tradition Since 1985

At Akapulko, we believe that great food brings people together. Founded by the Rodriguez family over three decades ago, our restaurant has been serving authentic Mexican recipes passed down through generations. Every dish is prepared with locally-sourced ingredients, traditional techniques, and a whole lot of passion.

Whether you're here for a quiet dinner, a celebration with friends, or a quick lunch, our warm atmosphere and attentive staff are dedicated to making your visit memorable. Come taste the difference that real Mexican cooking makes.
